Directions

CamLSAS2024 will be held at Cancer Research UK Cambridge Institute (CRUK-CI).
Address: Robinson Way, Cambridge CB2 0RE, UK
The main entrance of CRUK-CI is close to Puddicombe Way.

By Public Transportation:

    1. You can take A or U1/U2 bus services to Puddicombe Way.
    2. Several alternative bus services are available to take you to Addenbrooke's/Biomedical Campus, located just a 10-minute walk away from CRUK-CI.

By Cycle:

    1. Navigate through bike lanes and paths towards Puddicombe Way. There are bike racks available for parking near the Cancer Research UK Cambridge Institute.

By Car:

    1. We have reserved 10 on-site parking spots. Please indicate in the registration form if you require one.
